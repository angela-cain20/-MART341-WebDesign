The Grief Keeper

By Alexandra Villasante

The Grief Keeper is a YA novel surrounding the story of 17-year-old Marisol and her younger sister Gabi travelling across the border of El-Salvador to the United States to seek freedom and refuge away from gang violence. In her story, Marisol finds love and freedom in fellow troubled teen as they grapple with their contrasted world-views and experiences.
